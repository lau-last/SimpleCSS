‚öôÔ∏è Options

Add these optional classes on .table to change borders, spacing or striping.

.table-sm

Smaller cell padding.

.table-md

Medium padding (custom value).

.table-lg

Larger, more spacious rows.

.table-borderless

Removes all cell borders.

.table-bordered

Full grid borders on all cells.

.table-hover

Highlights a row on hover.

.table-striped

Stripes odd rows in tbody.

.table-striped-even

Stripes even rows in tbody.

.table-striped-columns

Stripes odd columns (head, body, foot).

.table-striped-columns-even

Stripes even columns.

.table-group-divider

Adds a strong divider above the first row in a group (apply on tbody).

.table-responsive

Wraps the table to enable horizontal scroll on small screens.
